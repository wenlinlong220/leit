**New filter promises clean water for millions**

*An ingenious invention is set to bring clean water to developing countries, and while the science may be cutting edge, the materials are extremely down to earth.*

**A** [[A handful of %%clay%% [Q14], yesterday’s coffee grounds and some cow manure are the ingredients...]] The simple new technology, developed by Tony Flynn, allows water filters to be made from commonly available materials and fired (or baked) using cow manure as the source of heat, without the need for a kiln.

**B** The invention was born out of a project involving the **Manatuto** community in East Timor. [[A charity operating there wanted to help %%set up a small industrial site manufacturing water filters%% [Q24], but initial research found the %%local clay to be too fine — a problem%% [Q20] solved by the addition of organic material.]] Flynn’s technique for manure firing... has made this zero-technology approach available anywhere it is needed.

**C** [[Take a handful of clay, mix it with a handful of organic material such as used tea leaves, coffee grounds or rice hulls, %%add water%% [Q15] in a sufficient quantity to make a stiff mixture...]] According to Flynn, used coffee grounds have given the **best results** to date [Q21]. The walls of the filter can be measured using the width of an adult finger as the standard. [[Next, surround the pots with %%straw%% [Q16], put them in a mound of %%cow manure%% [Q17], light the straw and then top up the burning manure as required. The filters are %%finished in 45 to 60 minutes%% [Q19].]]

**D** [[The %%properties%% of cow manure are vital, as the fuel can reach a temperature of 700 degrees in half an hour, and will be up to %%950 degrees%% [Q18] after %%another 20 to 30 minutes%% [Q22].]] The manure makes a good fuel because it is very high in organic material that burns readily and quickly. In contrast, a potter’s kiln is an expensive item...

**E** The filtration process is simple, but effective. [[The basic principle is that there are passages through the filter that are %%wide enough%% for water droplets... but %%too narrow%% for pathogens.]] [[Tests with the deadly %%E. coli%% [Q23] bacterium have seen the filters remove 96.4 to 99.8 per cent of the pathogen.]] [[The %%thickness%% of the clay container %%needs to be%% the same thickness as an adult finger %%for the process to be effective%% [Q25].]]

**F** With all the components being widely available, Flynn says there is no reason the technology couldn’t be applied throughout the developing world. [[He has %%no plans to exploit his idea financially%% by registering ownership through a patent. [Q26]]] [[If he %%did%%, any commercial copying %%would%% legally %%entitle%% him to a share in any profits %%made%%. Without a patent, there will be no illegality in it being %%adopted in any community%% that needs it.]]

**Questions 14–19 (Flow-chart)**

**Q14 & Q15: Make the mixture from organic material, 14.______ and 15.______**
- 定位词：mixture, organic material (tea, coffee, rice)
- 原文：Take a handful of **clay**, mix it with... add **water**...
- ✅ 答案：**clay / water** (顺序可交换)

**Q16 & Q17: Shape into pots and place them in a fire made from 16.______ and 17.______**
- 定位词：pots, fire made from
- 原文：...surround the pots with **straw**, put them in a mound of **cow manure**...
- ✅ 答案：**straw / cow manure** (顺序可交换)

**Q18: Fuel the fire to reach a maximum temperature of 18.______**
- 定位词：maximum temperature
- 原文：...will be up to **950 degrees** after another 20 to 30 minutes.
- ✅ 答案：**950 degrees**不小心填了另外一个数字

**Q19: Bake the filters in the fire for a maximum period of 19.______**
- 定位词：Bake, maximum period (==时间==)
- 原文：The filters are finished in 45 to **60 minutes**.
- 🧠 **错题笔记**：此题位于第3段末，而18题在第4段。流程图是按物理步骤出题，最后一步“完工时间”即使在文中靠前，也会排在最后。
- ✅ 答案：**60 minutes**

**Questions 20–23 (T/F/NG)**

**Q20: The clay in the Manatuto project was initially unsuitable...**
- 定位词：Manatuto, initially unsuitable
- 原文：...found the local clay to be **too fine — a problem**...
- 分析：`too fine` 被描述为 `a problem`，对应题目 `unsuitable`。
- ✅ 答案：**TRUE**

**Q21: Coffee grounds produce filters that are twice as efficient as those using other...**
- 定位词：Coffee grounds, twice as efficient
- 原文：...used coffee grounds have given the **best results** to date.
- 分析：原文**完全没提**“两倍（twice）”这一比例。
- ❓ 答案：**NOT GIVEN**

**Q22: It takes half an hour for a cow manure fire to reach 950 degrees.**
- 定位词：half an hour, 950 degrees
- 原文：...700 degrees in half an hour, and... 950 degrees **after another** 20 to 30 minutes.
- 分析：总共需要 30 + 20/30 分钟。题目说“半小时”与原文矛盾。
- ❌ 答案：**FALSE**

**Q23: E. coli is the most difficult bacterium to remove...**
- 定位词：E. coli, most difficult
- 原文：...remove 96.4 to 99.8 per cent of the pathogen.
- 分析：原文仅证实能移除 E. coli，**完全没提**它是否是“最难的”。
- ❓ 答案：**NOT GIVEN**

*## 核心长难句语法解析

### 1. 指令性祈使句并列
**原句：** Next, **surround** the pots with straw, **put** them in a mound of cow manure, **light** the straw and then **top up** the burning manure **as required**.
- **语法结构：** 典型的祈使句（Imperative Sentence），主语是省略的 `You`。
- **重点词汇：** `top up` (加满/补充)；`as required` (省略结构，意为“根据需要”，完整为 *as it is required*)。
- **解析：** 描述科学实验或手工步骤时，常用动词原形开头

### 2. 虚拟语气：对过去/现状的假设
**原句：** If he **did**, any commercial copying **would** legally **entitle** him to a share in any profits **made**.
- **语法结构：** 第二虚拟语气（Hypothetical Condition）。
- **解析：** `did` 是代动词（指申请专利）。作者假设如果他申请了，会有什么经济回报，衬托他现实中放弃专利的无私。
- **考点：** `entitle sb. to sth.` (使某人有权获得某物)；`made` (过去分词后置定语，修饰 profits)。

### 3. 动名词复合结构
**原句：** Without a patent, there will be no illegality in **it being adopted** in any community that needs it.
- **语法结构：** `it being adopted` 为动名词复合结构。
- **解析：** `it` 是逻辑主语（指代这项技术），`being adopted` 是动名词的被动式，整体作介词 `in` 的宾语。
- **理解：**总之就是如果他不申请专利，谁都能用这个

## 语言点

1. **Property <=> Characteristic/Nature** (n. 特性/属性) —— *熟词僻义，科学类文章高频*。
2. **Exploit financially** (短语. 从中渔利/利用其牟利)。
3. **A handful of** (词组. 一把/少量的)。
4. **manure** 粪便，肥料
5. **straw**稻草
6. **vital**至关重要的
7. **thickness**厚度
