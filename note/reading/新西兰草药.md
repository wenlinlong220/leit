## 错题梳理

- Q14：A reference to the interest shown by large corporations in herbal remedies.

- 原句：Natural health products are big sellers... and ==global pharmaceutical companies== are ==buying up== natural supplement brands or developing their own.

:::analysis
复盘：最初被 C 段的 "interest" 误导，但 C 段说的是医生和科学界（general practitioners and scientific community）缺乏兴趣。题目要求的是“大公司”（large corporations），对应 D 段的“全球制药公司（global pharmaceutical companies）”。

考点：Large corporations <=> global pharmaceutical companies（大企业 = 全球制药公司）；Interest <=> buying up / developing their own（商业收购与研发 = 表现出兴趣）。
:::

:::answer
Paragraph D
:::

- Q22：Restrictions on where you can buy.

- 原句：For example, consumers ==shouldn’t be able to go to their local supermarket and buy==... some herbs should ==require a prescription==.

:::analysis
复盘：没有迅速将“限制购买地点”对应到具体的“超市”。 Phil Rasmussen 认为有些药需要“处方（prescription）”，意味着不能在普通“超市”随便买，这属于对销售地点的限制。

考点：Restrictions on where you can buy <=> shouldn't be able to go to local supermarket / require a prescription（购买地点的限制 = 不能在超市买 / 需要处方）。
:::

:::answer
Phil Rasmussen (A)
:::

- Q23：The authorities stopped supporting one project without a good reason.

- 原句：However, the ==initiative== was ==stopped in its tracks== when the ==government abruptly halted funding== for the research programme. It was an ==unjustified move==... based on a number of ==inaccurate views==.

:::analysis
复盘：1. 词汇混淆：把 Authorities（当局/官方）误认为 Author（作者）。 2. 逻辑对应：没有把“没有好理由”对应到文中的“不合理/没根据（unjustified）”以及“错误的观点（inaccurate views）”。

考点：Authorities <=> government（官方 = 政府）；Stopped supporting <=> halted funding（停止支持 = 停止拨款）；Without a good reason <=> unjustified move / inaccurate views（无正当理由 = 不合理的举动/错误的看法）。
:::

:::answer
Malcolm Douglas (B)
:::

- Q24：The herbal medicine industry has an effect on the environment.

- 原句：‘The great majority of these are harvested from the wild, so they each have an ==impact on their local ecosystem==.’

:::analysis
复盘：需要进行近义词转换。题目中的“环境（environment）”在原文中对应的是更专业的词汇“生态系统（ecosystem）”；“影响（effect）”对应“冲击（impact）”。

考点：Effect <=> impact（影响/冲击）；Environment <=> local ecosystem（环境 = 当地生态系统）。
:::

:::answer
Isla Burgess (D)
:::

- Q25：New Zealand has the human resources and natural environment to grow herbs commercially.

- 原句：And today there is huge potential for New Zealand to develop a herb industry based on the ==excellent growing conditions and expertise== there.

:::analysis
复盘：考查抽象归纳能力。原文的“优越的生长条件（growing conditions）”属于“自然环境（natural environment）”，而“专门知识/技术（expertise）”属于“人力资源（human resources）”。

考点：Human resources <=> expertise（专业知识）；Natural environment <=> excellent growing conditions（自然环境 = 优越的生长条件）。
:::

:::answer
Phil Rasmussen (A)
:::

## 句子精析

1. However, the initiative was stopped in its tracks when the government abruptly halted funding for the research programme.
    initiative：倡议、计划；stopped in its tracks：原本指火车停在轨道上，引申为“半路夭折、戛然而止”。

2. For many years, the limited evidence [there was] [that herbal medicines worked] was undermined by lack of interest [among general practitioners and within the scientific community].
    [there was] 是定语从句，修饰 evidence，意为“现有的、所存在的证据”。
    [that herbal medicines worked] 是同位语从句，说明 evidence 的具体内容。
    undermined：原意是“在下面挖坑”，引申为“削弱、动摇……的根基”。
